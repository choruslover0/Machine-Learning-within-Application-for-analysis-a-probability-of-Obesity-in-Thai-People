# Product

## Register

product

## Users

Thai high-school students, professors, and curious members of the public who want to understand an educational obesity-risk probability estimate without needing technical knowledge.

## Product Purpose

O-Beast turns body and lifestyle survey answers into an ML-powered obesity-risk probability, then pairs the result with practical wellness advice grounded in trusted public-health guidance. Success means users understand the estimate, its limitations, and useful next actions.

## Brand Personality

Friendly, encouraging, curious. Precise enough to support a student research presentation, warm enough that users do not feel judged.

## Anti-references

- Sterile hospital interfaces
- Aggressive neon fitness apps
- Impersonal enterprise dashboards
- Technical diagrams that hide the user journey

## Design Principles

1. Explain predictions without claiming diagnosis.
2. Use calm, readable data visualization.
3. Separate data preparation from prediction algorithms.
4. Make research methods understandable to non-technical users.
5. Keep limitations visible.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

WCAG AA minimum. Support reduced motion. Never rely on color alone to communicate meaning. Use plain language suitable for students and general users.
